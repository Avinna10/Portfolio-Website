Technology

Technology is the application of scientific knowledge that is put into practical life in order to solve probelms and invent tools. Technology includes machines but also other techniques and processes to create chips for those machines. Technology helps people and determines how people interact with each other. Technology impacts in everyday lives and have both positive and negative aspects. Today's modern world is full with technology. It is an era of technology.



Impacts of Technology

Over the years, technology has revolutionized our world. Technology impacts in our lives daily either positively or negatively. Technology affects the way we communicate, learn and think. Technology plays an important role in our society and impacts our daily lives. Advancement in technology is common in context of present world. Internet is one of the example.

As in the learning process, technology have made it more interactive and collaborative. With the help of technology, we can engage with our teachers and friends and learn without any trouble. The use of internet has made us more accessible to learn more external and deep knowledges. We can have access to anything online 24-hr. As a student, technology have helped us to get our work done easier. Many informative videos and researches can be viewed easily, we can also focus on self-learning during leisure. These advancement in technologies have made learning process more fun and convenient.

Similarly, technology has impacted us in communication too. Technology has brought many new methods for the communication which are fast and convenient. There are email, social networking sites and video conferencing through which we can communicate easily. As advancement of technology, we have more options to communicate either by texting or video chatting. No matter how far we are form each other, modern technology has helped us to communicate easily within a second.

However the technology advances it leaves some negative effects too. As because of virtual communication and spending most of the time surfing internet, relation between people can decrease. Giving more time to technology also disturbs the education and school/college works. Every thing is open about ourselves to the world in the internet so there is less privacy. In conclusion, how technology affects our daily lives depend on how we use them. Without the advancement of technologies, our life would have been more complex.
